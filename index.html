Polar Breach

My latest project is an upcoming dungeon-crawling action game set in an Antarctic lab, where you fight aliens using magnetic powers. I have written dozens of design document pages and created various spreadsheets to maintain the game's vision, and have written 200+ lines of dialogue for the story. It's by far my most ambitious project yet. Stay tuned!

Side Job Steve

What if a bounty hunter turned to UberEats? This is how we took the theme "branching out" for a 2-week game jam. I designed the game loop, which took the arcade classic Space Harrier and added 3 side jobs to complete and upgrades to purchase along the way. I also drew and implemented all of the game's graphics! This game won 3rd place overall in the jam vote.

Underpromotion

We worked within the constraints of RPG Maker to make a chess-inspired puzzle game. As lead designer, I designed a paper prototype for the concept, then further refined the movement mechanics, progression system, and level designs, and helped playtests. As lead writer, I wrote a 4-page lore bible and 190+ lines of dialogue. These designs won us the university's annual award for Outstanding Mechanics.
