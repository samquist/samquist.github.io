About

I'm a game designer, artist, and writer, currently studying while making as many games as possible. I've worked on various indie projects in Unity, Unreal Engine, and RPG Maker. My passion has always been creating fun and unique stories and experiences, and I'm eager to learn new skills and take on fresh challenges.
