Games

Polar Breach

Top-Down Action | Godot | Game Off 2025

Collaboration with an international team for Game Off 2025. I was responsible for combat design, including the player's moveset and combo system. I designed enemy behavior, where I learned a lot about hierarchical finite state machines, and also wrote the story, including 200+ lines of dialogue for the script.

Side Job Steve

Rail Shooter | Unity | Alberta Slow Jam 2025

Made across 2 weeks by a small Albertan dev team. I was responsible for the core game loop, which referenced the arcade classic Space Harrier. I also designed 3 side jobs and an upgrade system, and drew and implemented all of the game's graphics! This game won 3rd place overall in the jam vote.

Underpromotion

Puzzle Adventure | RPG Maker | Return 0

Student project at the University of Alberta. We worked within the constraints of RPG Maker to design a chess-inspired puzzle game, first proving the concept with paper prototypes. I conducted playtests and wrote 190+ lines of dialogue. My design won us the school's annual award for Outstanding Mechanics.

Narrative Analysis

Sonic '06

In this almost hour-long epic, I analyze the character of Silver the Hedgehog from the maligned Sonic the Hedgehog (2006). I delve into both the game's ludic and literary mechanics to paint a full picture of Silver's character. It is my most popular analysis video, thanks to the series' passionate fans.

Maverick Hunter X

Here, I study the posthumanist themes within Mega Man Maverick Hunter X and the overall Mega Man X series using academic sources. Even games made for kids can be full of rich, thought-provoking themes like this.

Metal Gear Solid V

As part of a university class, I analyzed Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain to undestand how the game teaches the player how languages are suppressed using its game mechanics. The project got me an A+, so that's gotta be worth something.
