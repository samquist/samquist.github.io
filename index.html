An emerging game designer crafting award-winning games that are as innovative as they are full of personality. I thrive on turning creative challenges into unique experiences that tell great stories and are lots of fun. Whether prototyping puzzles or perfecting a platformer, I have a keen eye for design and a relentless drive to learn and improve.

Games

Polar Breach

Top-Down Action | Godot | Game Off 2025

Collaboration with an international team for Game Off 2025. I was responsible for combat design, documenting the player's moveset and combo system. I designed varied enemy behavior using finite state machines. I also wrote the story, including 200+ lines of dialogue for the script.

Side Job Steve

Rail Shooter | Unity | Alberta Slow Jam 2025

Made across 2 weeks by a small Albertan dev team. I was responsible for the core game loop, which referenced the arcade classic Space Harrier. I also designed 3 side jobs and an upgrade system, and drew and implemented all of the game's graphics. This game won 3rd place overall in the jam vote.

Underpromotion

Puzzle Adventure | RPG Maker | Return 0

Student project at the University of Alberta. We created a fully-realized puzzle game using RPG Maker MV, first proving the concept with paper prototypes. I conducted playtests and wrote 180+ lines of dialogue. We won the school's annual award for Outstanding Mechanics.

Narrative Analysis

Sonic '06

In this almost hour-long video, I analyze the character of Silver the Hedgehog from the maligned Sonic the Hedgehog (2006). I delve into both the game's ludic and literary mechanics to paint a full picture of this character. It is my most popular analysis video.

Maverick Hunter X

Here, I study the posthumanist themes within Mega Man Maverick Hunter X and the overall Mega Man X series using academic sources. Even games made for kids can be full of rich, thought-provoking themes like this.

Metal Gear Solid V

As part of a university class, I analyzed Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain to undestand how the game teaches the player how languages are suppressed using its game mechanics. The project got me an A+, so that's gotta be worth something.
